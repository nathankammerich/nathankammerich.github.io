Welcome to the About Page

My name is Nathan Kammerich, and I am a senior at the University of Missouri where I study information technology. My biggest passions in life are music and technology. Ever since I was a kid, I’ve had a love for anything related to technology. This includes video games, computers, the internet, etc.

I took this class because I’ve always been interested in web design, and after finishing this class I’ll meet all the requirements for an additional certification with my degree. I love to listen to music, and I also enjoy making it, I play both the saxophone and the drums. I grew up in Columbia Missouri, so I’ve been here my whole life so far. I figured out pretty quickly as a child that I wanted to go to Mizzou because I wouldn’t have to pay out-of-state tuition, and it had a lot of ways I could continue to play music while also furthering my career.
